Community.
Task templates from the public Vigil community repo. Fork any of them into your library.
Templates are synced from Susquehanna-Syntax/Vigil-Approved-Scripts and reviewed by PR before they land. Fork opens a template in your editor — nothing runs without your review, and deploying still requires 2FA. Share your own from the task editor with Submit to Community.
